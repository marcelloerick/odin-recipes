Chicken Noodles Recipe

Description

Chicken noodles are a traditional Indonesian dish of boiled wheat noodles adapted from the bakmi cuisine brought by Chinese immigrants. The dish was modified to include soy sauce-marinated chicken pieces and chicken fat to make it halal and appeal to the palates of the local people of the Indonesian archipelago. Today, this legendary street food is extremely popular because it combines chewy noodles, savory-sweet chicken, and fresh vegetables in one delicious bowl.

Ingredients

  • 500 g egg noodles
  • 300 g chicken, cut into strips
  • 500 mL chicken fat
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 shallots
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 finger-length piece of turmeric
  • 4 candlenuts
  • 1 finger-length piece of ginger
  • 2 L chicken broth
  • 1 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Chicken bones, as needed

Instructions

  1. First, make the chicken noodle broth. To do this, bring the chicken cooking liquid back to a boil and add ground pepper, salt, and chicken bones.
  2. For the chicken topping, boil the chicken first, then cut it into small pieces or mince it finely.
  3. Grind the spices—such as shallots, garlic, coriander, turmeric, candlenuts, and ginger—into a paste
  4. Heat the oil, then sauté the chicken with the spice paste until cooked through.
  5. Add salt and seasoning to taste.
  6. Once the chicken noodle broth and toppings are ready, boil the prepared egg noodles.
  7. Prepare a serving bowl, then add 1 tbsp of chicken oil and 1 tsp of black pepper.
  8. Place the cooked noodles into the serving bowl and toss until the noodles are well coated with the chicken oil.
  9. Add the chicken topping and the chicken broth.
  10. Your simple chicken noodles are ready to serve.
